How do I pay?

You can pay cash, online, through tickets, or by arrangement.
We offer the following arrangement options:
Prepaid | starting from R2 per errand
Weekly | starting from R50 per week (10 Errands)
Monthly | starting from R150 per month (30 Errands)

How does phathisa Errand servce charge?

Each errand runner adds their price on top of our base fee. Our fees are as follows:
- We charge R2 per errand less than 10 minutes.
- We charge R7 per errand over 15 minutes.
- We charge R60 per errand less than or equal to 1 hour.

How do I sign-up to work as an errand runner

Becoming a Phathisa errand runner starts with creating an account, and vetting takes 3 days.
